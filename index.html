Why Wayve?


For decades Los Angeles has been known for a burgeoning entertainment industry, where musicians, writers, artists, filmmakers, and actors come together to materialize their dreams. They master their skills like scientists and artfully deliver those unique qualities into the world, providing a better experience for all. Sounds a lot like entrepreneurs? We bet it does, because they ARE. The spirit of entrepreneurship in LA has been prominent long before the recent tech boom.

With companies like SnapChat, SpaceX, and TrueCar, Los Angeles has been recognized as not only one of the best, but also one of the fastest growing tech hubs in the world. Our region graduates the most engineers in the country and is home to institutions like USC, Caltech, Harvey Mudd, and UCLA, which have been fueling innovation for decades. Los Angeles is well positioned to become the strongest tech hub in the country, but we must start engaging with our young talent and university institutions if we wish to get there. Remember, greatness starts early.
